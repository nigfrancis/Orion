Banking and Finance

The financial services sector has undergone a dramatic transformation in recent years - driven by regulatory changes and the aggressive adoption of information technology. Players within financial services markets must constantly innovate to remain at the forefront of the competitive landscape.

In the financial services industry, firms differentiate themselves from their competitors through the enhancement of the services they render to their customers. Orion Solutions works with clients to rethink the value proposition they are presenting to their clients, resulting in service innovation and expansion of service delivery opportunities.

Orion Solutions professionals have worked in and consulted to organizations across the financial services spectrum, including banks, independent money managers, brokerage houses, and private equity firms.
