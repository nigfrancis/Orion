Human Capital Management Services

It is an established truism that people are the greatest asset of any business. What is less frequently acknowledged is the inherent challenge of effectively managing personnel to ensure an optimal mix of skills and knowledge within an organization. Orion Solutions has an extensive track record of supporting the human capital needs of our clients, including the first hand management of training and job placements programs for both public and private sector entities.

We employ a holistic approach to the human capital needs of our clients. Skill development and opportunities for learning are not meant to be isolated events, pursued once or twice a year. Rather, the development and refinement of skills and organizational knowledge is a continuous process which must stand at the center of a firm's pursuit of competitive advantage. The Orion Solutions method includes attention to the following facets of human capital management:

  • Skill Development & Career Planning
  • Performance Measurement & Appraisal
  • Organizational Skill Auditing & Needs Assessment
  • Knowledge Management
  • Training Content Development
  • Collaborative Learning Opportunities
  • Customer Education
  • Distributed Learning Resources
