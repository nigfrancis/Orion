Innovation in a Rapidly Changing Market

The industries of health care and the life sciences are at the cutting edge of some of the greatest technological and process innovations of the age. However, businesses within these fields are also experiencing a period of tremendous change. The management of costs and the effective delivery of services present challenges that are without precedent in the history of the field.

Orion Solutions has extensive experience serving clients on all sides of the health care equation:

  • Managed care organizations and other payer institutions
  • Hospitals and clinical care providers
  • Life sciences firms
  • Healthcare information technology
  • Pharmacy benefits management

"Questions of morality, government intervention, and the cost-benefit tradeoff between health and illness are the issues facing the nation in the new millennium of healthcare ."

 

- Coile & Trusko, "Healthcare 2020: Technology in the New Millennium"
